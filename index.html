Our story

About Aurella crochet

Aurella crochet began with a single hook, a ball of yarn, and a habit of turning quiet evenings into something beautiful. What started as a small creative outlet slowly grew into a passion — and that passion is now growing into a business.

Every piece we make carries a little bit of that original joy: the satisfaction of watching a single strand become a cardigan, a clip, a gajra, a keychain. Our goal is simple — to build Aurella crochet into a full-time handmade brand, one happy customer at a time, without ever losing the care and attention that handmade work deserves.

We believe in quality over quantity, in slow-made over fast fashion, and in making sure that whoever receives an Aurella crochet piece feels how much thought went into it.
